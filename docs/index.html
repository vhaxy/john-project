About Me

I'm John and I am in my eighth year of teaching and tutoring maths. I worked in broadcast journalism for a long time but made the switch into teaching because of a desire to help young people reach their potential. I started teaching maths because I found that I understood why some people struggle, or just think they are bad at it.
